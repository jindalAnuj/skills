## Composition Rules

- **Rule of Thirds** — `"subject positioned on the left third of the frame"`
- **Leading Lines** — `"perspective lines converging toward the subject"`
- **Foreground Layering** — `"blurred foliage in foreground, subject in midground, fog-filled valley in background"`
- **Negative Space** — `"subject dwarfed by vast empty sky, minimalist composition"`
- **Symmetry** — `"perfectly centered symmetrical composition"`
- **Dutch Angle** — `"Dutch tilt 15 degrees, tension and unease"`

## Lighting — Full Reference

### Technique Details

**Chiaroscuro**
Strong contrast, deep shadows. Origins in Renaissance painting. Single hard source, shadow occupying 60–70% of frame.
> `"dramatic chiaroscuro lighting, single hard light source, deep shadows engulfing 70% of frame"`

**Rembrandt Lighting**
Triangle of light on the shadowed cheek.
> `"Rembrandt lighting, three-quarter face, shadow with light triangle on cheek"`

**Butterfly Lighting**
Overhead front light, shadow directly below nose. Hollywood glamour.
> `"butterfly lighting, soft overhead front key, glamorous Hollywood portrait"`

**Rim / Edge Lighting**
Light behind subject creates glowing edge.
> `"strong rim light from behind, backlit with glowing edge, separated from background"`

**Split Lighting**
Exactly half face in light, half in shadow.
> `"split lighting, half-face illumination, mystery and duality"`

**Volumetric Lighting / God Rays**
Light made visible through particles.
> `"volumetric light rays cutting through fog"`, `"god rays through forest canopy"`

**Caustics**
Refracted light patterns through glass or water.
> `"caustic light patterns on the floor through a glass window"`

**Silhouette**
Subject fully backlit, no fill.
> `"complete silhouette against fiery sunset, no fill light"`

## Lens & Focal Length — Full Reference

| Focal Length | Effect | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| 14–24mm | Exaggerated depth, ultra-wide | Landscapes, interiors, dramatic wides |
| 35mm | Natural human vision | Documentary, street, intimate |
| 50mm | Neutral, no distortion | Versatile |
| 85mm | Subject isolation, slight compression | Portrait, character close-up |
| 135mm | Strong compression, shallow DOF | Environmental portrait, romance |
| 200mm+ | Heavy compression, telephoto | Wildlife, emotional distance |
| Macro | Extreme close-up of tiny detail | Textures, insects, abstraction |

### Lens Effects

- **Anamorphic**: `"anamorphic lens flare, oval bokeh, 2.39:1 aspect ratio"` — horizontal flares, letterbox
- **Bokeh**: `"creamy circular bokeh orbs, f/1.4, shallow DOF"` — point lights become glowing circles
- **Chromatic aberration**: `"slight chromatic aberration on edges"` — lo-fi, vintage
- **Film grain**: `"heavy 35mm film grain"`, `"Kodak Portra film grain"`, `"subtle analog grain"`

### Camera Rigs
