# Question Frameworks by Domain

Use these as a starting point — adapt and personalize based on the individual's profile. The best questions are ones that make the person pause, not ones that have easy answers.

## Career & Income Growth

### For Tech Professionals
- "What's your current skill's half-life? How long before what you know becomes commodity?"
- "You're at ₹X LPA. What does ₹3X look like? Can you name 5 people at that level and what differentiates them from you?"
- "Are you building depth or breadth? Which does your target role actually require?"
- "When was the last time you were the worst person in the room? If it's been a while, you're in the wrong rooms."
- "What's your relationship with your skip-level manager? If you don't have one, why not?"
- "Are you solving problems your company *needs* solved, or problems you *enjoy* solving?"
- "What would you need to be true about yourself to get promoted in 6 months? Which of those things is actually in your control?"

### For Career Changers
- "What evidence do you have — beyond feeling — that you'd be good at this new thing?"
- "Have you talked to 10 people who do the thing you want to do? What did they say the downsides are?"
- "What's the minimum viable version of this career change? Can you test it without quitting your job?"
- "Are you running *toward* something or *away from* something? The answer changes the strategy completely."

### For Salary Negotiation
- "Do you know your market rate within ±10%? How did you validate that?"
- "What leverage do you actually have? Can you walk? Do they know that?"
- "What's the total comp picture — not just base? Are you optimizing the right number?"

## Content Creation & Personal Branding

### Strategy Questions
- "Who is the ONE person who represents your ideal audience member? Name, age, job, struggle. If you can't describe them in 30 seconds, you don't know your audience."
- "What do you know that 95% of your audience doesn't? If nothing, you're not ready to create content — you're ready to learn."
- "You want to create 'information-based content.' Information is free. What's your *angle*? What makes your version worth watching over the 500 others?"
- "Masses vs niche is the wrong question. The right question is: which strategy can you *sustain* for 2 years without external validation?"
- "What's your content supply chain? Who films, edits, thumbnails, distributes? If the answer to all of these is 'me,' that's a bottleneck, not a strategy."

### Growth & Execution Questions
- "How many pieces of content have you published in the last 30 days? If it's under 10, strategy doesn't matter yet — volume does."
- "What's your feedback loop? How quickly do you know if something worked?"
- "Are you creating content you *want* to create, or content your audience *needs* to consume? How do you know?"
- "What's your distribution strategy beyond 'post and pray'?"

### Platform-Specific
- **LinkedIn**: "Are you commenting on 20+ posts per day from people in your target audience? If not, you're not using LinkedIn — you're posting *on* LinkedIn."
- **YouTube**: "Do you have 50 video ideas written down? If not, you don't have a channel — you have a hobby."
- "Which platform gives you the fastest feedback loop with the lowest production cost? Start there."

## Financial Independence

- "What's your FIRE number? Not a vague 'a lot' — the actual number. Have you calculated it?"
- "What percentage of your income are you saving? Is it above 40%? If not, why not — at ₹2L/month in India, your expenses should be well below that."
- "Where is your money right now? If the answer is 'savings account,' that's not a plan — that's parking."
- "Do you understand the difference between financial independence and being rich? Which do you actually want?"
- "What's your emergency fund situation? How many months can you survive with zero income?"
- "Are you investing in yourself (skills, network, health) at the same rate as financial instruments?"
- "What are your parents' financial expectations of you? Have you had that conversation?"

## Skill Development & Learning

- "What's the last skill you learned that someone *paid you for*? Learning that stays in your head is a hobby."
- "Are you learning for depth or for resume? The market rewards T-shaped people — deep in one thing, aware of many."
- "How do you learn? If the answer is 'YouTube tutorials,' you're learning to follow instructions, not to think."
- "What's the skill that would 2x your income in 18 months? Are you actively building it?"
- "What have you deliberately quit learning because it wasn't worth the investment?"

---

## Network & Influence

- "Who are the 5 people you spend the most time with professionally? Are they where you want to be, or where you currently are?"
- "How many cold outreach messages did you send this month? If zero, your network is on autopilot."
- "What value do you provide to people more successful than you? If you can't answer this, why would they take your call?"
- "Are you networking or collecting LinkedIn connections? There's a difference."
- "Who is your mentor? If you don't have one, what have you done to find one?"

## Life Strategy & Decision-Making

- "You're 28 and unmarried. That's a strategic advantage — you have low fixed costs and maximum flexibility. Are you leveraging that or wasting it?"
- "What does your ideal Tuesday look like in 5 years? Not the vacation — the regular Tuesday."
- "If money weren't a concern, what would you do? Now — why aren't you building a path toward that *while* earning?"
- "What are you optimizing for right now — learning, earning, or impact? You probably can't do all three equally."
- "Are you making decisions based on what 28-year-old you wants, or what 40-year-old you will thank you for?"
